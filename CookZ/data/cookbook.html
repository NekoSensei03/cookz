General Cooking Instructions

You can cook using a pot, cauldron or pan. This book describes how you can combine ingredients to create nutritious dishes. If you do not follow the instructions correctly then the ingredients are cooked individually. Some dishes need packaging material like empty cans or boxes attached to the cooking equipment. Some dishes need water or a specific cooking equipment.

Packaging Materials

You can craft ten empty cans from a metal sheet using a hacksaw.

An empty box can be crafted from two pieces of paper or by using any axe or knife on bark.





Boxed Dishes

Fried Potatoes: 4x potato, 1x lard. Fry in a pan. Fill into an empty box.

Stir Fry: 1x potato, 1x tomato, 1x zucchini, 1x green pepper bell, 1x lard. Fry in a pan. Fill into an empty box.

Fish and Chips: 2x potato, 1x any fish fillet, 1x lard. Fry in a pan. Fill into an empty box.

Rice Pudding: 1x rice, 1x powdered milk, 2x any fruit. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty box.

Mushroom Risotto: 1x rice, 2x any mushroom. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty box.

Pilaf: 1x rice, 2x green pepper bell, 2x tomato. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty box.

Stuffed Peppers: 3x green pepper bell, 2x any meat. Bake in a pot or cauldron. Fill into an empty box.

Gagh: 8x worm. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty box.

Haggis with Mashed Potatoes: 1x guts, 2x potato. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty box.

Pommdoener: 1x beef steak, 2x potato, 1x lard. Fry in a pan. Fill into an empty box.

Bangers and Mash: 2x beef steak, 2x potato. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty box.


Canned Dishes

Goulash: 2x potato, 1x green pepper bell, 1x any meat. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty can.

Pumpkin Soup: 2x sliced pumpkin. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty can.

Fish Soup: 2x any fish, 1x potato, 1x tomato. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty can.

Ukha: 2x any fish, 1x potato, 1x tomato, 1x alcohol. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty can.

Lecso: 2x green pepper bell, 2x tomato, 1x lard. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty can.

Tactical Bacon: 2x pork steak, 1x lard. Fry in a pan or pot or cauldron. Fill into an empty can.

Mystery Meat: 3x any meat, 1x lard. Fry in a pan or pot or cauldron. Fill into an empty can.

Zucchini Caviar: 3x zucchini, 1x green pepper bell, 1x tomato. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty can.

Creamy Tomato Soup: 6x tomato, 1x powdered milk. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty can.

Beef Stew: 1x beef steak, 1x green pepper bell, 1x potato, 2x tomato. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty can.

Mushroom Stroganoff: 1x powdered milk, 5x any mushroom. Boil in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water. Fill into an empty can.

Apple Marmalade: 6x apple. Boil in a pot or cauldron (without water). Fill into an empty can.

Plum Marmalade: 6x plum. Boil in a pot or cauldron (without water). Fill into an empty can.

Pear Marmalade: 4x pear. Boil in a pot or cauldron (without water). Fill into an empty can.


Sausages

Beef Sausages: 1x guts, 1x beef steak. Put all in a pot or cauldron (without water).

Chicken Sausages: 1x guts, 3x chicken steak. Put all in a pot or cauldron (without water).

Pig Sausages: 1x guts, 2x pork steak. Put all in a pot or cauldron (without water).

Goat Sausages: 1x guts, 2x chevon steak. Put all in a pot or cauldron (without water).

Bear Sausages: 1x guts, 1x bear steak. Put all in a pot or cauldron (without water).

Sheep Sausages: 1x guts, 2x mutton steak. Put all in a pot or cauldron (without water).

Boar Sausages: 1x guts, 2x boar steak. Put all in a pot or cauldron (without water).

Deer Sausages: 1x guts, 2x vension steak. Put all in a pot or cauldron (without water).

Hare Sausages: 1x guts, 2x hare leg. Put all in a pot or cauldron (without water).

Wolf Sausages: 1x guts, 2x wolf steak. Put all in a pot or cauldron (without water).


Miscellaneous

Disinfect Rags: Boil rags in a pot or cauldron with at least 500 ml of water (better use more to keep it boiling) to disinfect them.
